About Me

My Story

Front end web developer making my way into the development world. Recently I earned a certificate in full stack development through Vanderbilt University, skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node, and many more packages to supplement these languages. I thoroughly enjoy client-side development, however, since I got introduced to the server side, that has drawn me in as well. I enjoy working with clients and seeing their happy faces when their dream can become a reality. Working in a team and solo environment has taught me version control, how to be incredibly organized with the assistance of Trello, and how to effectively communicate with my team. Many of my colleagues have commented on my optimism and joy while coding with them. I take great pride in what I do, and I strive to get others excited about it in the future.
